How to Play

Match Elements

Drag and drop elements to match them with similar blocks. Combine elements to create new ones and clear the board.

Solve Puzzles

Each level presents unique challenges. Think strategically to solve puzzles with the minimum number of moves.

Progress & Unlock

Complete levels to unlock new elements and challenges. Track your progress and aim for the highest score.

About Element Blocks

Element Blocks is a captivating puzzle game that challenges players to combine different elements to create new blocks and clear the board. With its intuitive gameplay and increasing difficulty, it offers a perfect balance of casual fun and brain-teasing challenges.

The game draws inspiration from classic match-three puzzles but adds a unique twist with its element combination mechanics. Players must think strategically about each move to optimize their score and complete levels efficiently.

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming session during your break or a longer puzzle-solving experience, Element Blocks adapts to your playing style and provides endless entertainment.
